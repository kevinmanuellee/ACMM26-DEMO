📡 Protocol

This standalone page mirrors the Memory Duel structure but adds immediate per-clip questions on top of frame-locked attention capture plus a reference overlay review.

1. Enter your participant ID or email.
2. Complete a face-alignment check immediately after entry.
3. Watch the Apple Watch, BYD Seal, and KFC clips in sequence.
4. Immediately after each clip, answer its multiple-choice question before the next clip starts.
5. For every clip, one attention snapshot is saved with a coarse left, center, or right estimate.
6. After each question, the review screen shows your locked frame with the gaze band on the left and the matching overlay frame on the right before the next clip begins.
